Case Studies

Challenge: A mid-sized eCommerce brand struggled with low ROAS from their Google Ads campaigns.

Approach: I conducted a full audit of their ad accounts, reorganized campaigns, optimized targeting, and implemented bid strategies. I also built dashboards in Looker Studio to track KPIs in real time.

Results: Within 3 months, ROAS improved by 45%, conversions increased by 30%, and campaign budgets were fully optimized to reduce waste.

Challenge: An online retailer had high traffic but low checkout completion rates.

Approach: I analyzed the sales funnel, conducted testing on checkout flows, optimized the cart experience, and implemented upsell/cross-sell strategies.

Results: Average order value (AOV) increased by 20%, cart abandonment dropped by 15%, and overall conversion rates improved by 25%, resulting in a measurable increase in revenue.

Challenge: A startup business website had poor visibility on Google and minimal organic traffic.

Approach: I performed a comprehensive technical SEO audit, optimized on-page content, built a keyword strategy, and implemented structured data. Google Search Console and GA4 were used to track improvements.

Results: Organic traffic increased by 70% in six months, key product pages ranked on the first page, and lead generation improved significantly.

Challenge: Analytics & Operational Efficiency

Approach: I automated data collection using Google Sheets, GA4, GTM, and built custom Looker Studio dashboards. Created SOPs for reporting processes.

Results: Reporting time was reduced by 50%, from 2 weeks to 1 week. KPIs were tracked in real time, and decision-making became data-driven, improving campaign performance and operational efficiency.
